What is Keating?

🎓

Diagnosis First

Before teaching, Keating understands what you already know and where your gaps lie. No wasted time on concepts you've mastered.

🧠

Reconstruction Over Regurgitation

Make you reconstruct ideas from memory, not merely agree with explanations. Real learning requires effort.

🚀

Transfer Testing

Ask you to carry ideas into new settings to prove genuine understanding. Confirmation isn't comprehension.

🗣️

Voice Preservation

Penalize rote echoing. Reward novel analogies and personal articulation. Your voice matters, not the AI's.

How It Works

1

You Ask a Question

Ask about any topic—math, philosophy, science, code, anything. Keating doesn't lecture, it investigates.

2

Keating Diagnoses

Instead of answering, Keating probes your current understanding. What's solid? What's shaky? Where are the gaps?

3

You reconstruct

Guided by questions, you rebuild the concept yourself. Struggling is the point—it's how neural pathways form.

4

Keating Tests Transfer

Apply the concept to a new context. Real understanding shows when you can use knowledge in unfamiliar situations.

Install Keating

$ curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Diogenesoftoronto/keating/main/scripts/install/install.sh | bash
